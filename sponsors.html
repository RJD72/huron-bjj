Our Sponsorship Team

We want to extend our heartfelt thanks to our incredible 2024 sponsors! Your support is the backbone of our academy, making a real difference in the lives of our athletes and community.

A portion of your generous sponsorship helps our competitive team travel across North America to compete, offering them invaluable experience and the chance to showcase their skills on a larger stage. This opportunity is crucial for their growth and success in the sport.

We are proud to recognize our 2024 Competitive Team Sponsors: Sky Ranch, Boston Pizza Goderich, The Cannabis Guys Goderich, Kwik Bite / Shell Goderich, and J&J Welding and Repair.

Additionally, your contributions fuel our Youth Mentorship Fund, ensuring that every child has the chance to train, regardless of their financial situation. A special thank you to Compass Minerals Goderich and our private sponsor for making this possible.

You're not just sponsoring a gym; you're empowering young athletes to reach their full potential and creating lifelong opportunities. Thank you for being an essential part of our journey!
