Welcome to Huron BJJ

Huron BJJ is the premier Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy in Goderich, offering expert instruction for kids, teens, and adults in a state-of-the-art facility. We have two IBJJF-certified black belts, we provide a safe, structured, and welcoming environment for all skill levels.

Whether you’re looking for high-level competition training—with numerous provincial champions in our ranks—or simply a fun, engaging way to stay active, we have a place for you. From kids learning self-defense to adults in their 60s and 70s enjoying the journey, our gym is built for everyone.

Whatever your goals, Huron BJJ has what you’re looking for. Try a free class today and experience the difference.
