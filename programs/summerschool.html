Summer Camp

Join the Huron BJJ Summer Camp Party!

Our summer camp in Goderich has been a fantastic experience year after year, open to both members and non-members. While we’re still finalizing the details for the upcoming camp, we’re excited to build on the incredible activities and partnerships we’ve enjoyed in the past.

Here’s a look at what we’ve offered in previous years:

  • Jiu-Jitsu Basics: Campers have learned self-defense, takedowns, submissions, and escapes, providing an engaging introduction to the sport.
  • Lunch: Thanks to local favorites like West Street Willy’s and Domino’s Goderich for keeping our campers fueled.
  • Visits: In the past, Victim Services Huron County has spoken about healthy relationships and consent, and the OPP has joined us to engage with our students.
  • Snacks: Daily snacks generously provided by Zehrs Goderich.
  • Adventures: Campers have enjoyed time at the park and splash pad.
  • Courses: Custom obstacle courses have challenged campers to push their limits.
  • DodgeBall: A fun, daily favorite activity.
  • Strength & Conditioning: Workhorse Strength & Conditioning has put campers through exciting fitness challenges to improve strength and endurance
  • Fitness Testing: Campers have tested their muscles, speed, and endurance with daily activities.
  • Movie Time: Relaxing with a movie on our 120” projector screen during lunch.
  • Crafts and Games: Organized activities to keep campers entertained and engaged.

We’re always striving to make the camp better and more exciting each year. Stay tuned for details—dates to be announced! Contact us today to join our email list for updates.
