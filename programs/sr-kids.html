Sr Kids

Ages 9 - 12

FOCUSING ON CREATING DISCIPLINED, SKILLED AND RESPECTFUL MARTIAL ARTISTS

Introducing our Senior Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Program at Huron BJJ—a transformative journey for individuals seeking to enhance their physical and mental well-being. We believe that BJJ training is not only a sport but also a lifestyle that fosters growth and resilience. Our program offers structured sessions providing a consistent schedule for our senior participants. Led by experienced instructors, these sessions focus on refining techniques, improving grappling skills, and enhancing overall fitness levels.

Technique and Teamwork

Program Description:

Tailored for individuals aged 9 and above, our Senior BJJ program delves deeper into advanced Jiu-Jitsu techniques and strategies. Participants engage in dynamic drills, live sparring sessions, and positional training, honing their abilities in a supportive environment. Led by Head Coach Shaun, our program follows the IBJJF grading system, ensuring adherence to industry standards. After each session, participants have the opportunity to engage in open mat sessions or review techniques with fellow practitioners, fostering camaraderie and mutual growth.

Champions in the Making

Program goals:

  • Develop advanced grappling techniques and strategies.
  • Enhance physical fitness, strength, and endurance.
  • Cultivate mental resilience and discipline.

This program is suitable for children who wish to pursue Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu recreationally or competitively. For those interested in competition, we offer free extra competitive classes for kids at a more advanced level, providing additional training and support to help them excel in tournaments.

Join our Senior BJJ Program at Huron BJJ and embark on a journey of personal growth and self-discovery. Whether you're a seasoned practitioner or new to the sport, our program provides a welcoming community where you can challenge yourself, learn new skills, and forge lasting friendships.
