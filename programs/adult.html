Adult

Beginner Adult Jiu-Jitsu Class

In our beginner adult jiu-jitsu class, students learn the essential techniques and foundations of jiu-jitsu in a supportive environment. With a strong focus on developing skills, teamwork, and discipline, our beginners quickly become part of the gym family. The confidence and camaraderie you see in this group are built over hours of dedicated practice. Whether you're completely new or have some martial arts experience, this class will help you improve your physical and mental strength.

All-Level Adult No-Gi Class

Our no-gi classes cater to students of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. The fast-paced, submission-oriented training in this group helps students refine their technique, improve their endurance, and elevate their overall jiu-jitsu game. Here, our members train hard and push each other to grow while building a tight-knit community that supports one another's development. This class emphasizes fluid movement, technique mastery, and adaptability, making it a perfect fit for anyone seeking to challenge themselves.

Adult Jiu-Jitsu Competition Team

At our gym, we don't just train; we compete. Our adult competition team takes pride in their dedication to training and their accomplishments in tournaments. Competing at a high level requires more than just skill; it takes heart, discipline, and a relentless work ethic. This team consistently shows up, pushing past limits and bringing home medals from regional and national competitions. If you're looking to take your jiu-jitsu to the next level and test your skills in competition, our team is ready to support you in reaching your goals.

Joining Huron BJJ today is a step towards becoming stronger, more confident, and skilled in self-defense. You'll start learning techniques from the moment you step on the mat and begin your journey in a supportive environment that prioritizes growth and camaraderie. Get started today and take the first step towards mastering Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu!
