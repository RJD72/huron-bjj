Shaun Garrity

Shaun, the head coach at Huron BJJ, has been an invaluable asset to our academy. With many years of dedicated practice and numerous competition victories under his belt, Shaun brings a wealth of experience and expertise to our team. His coaching philosophy revolves around the importance of mastering technique and precision, while also fostering a supportive and inclusive community for all our students. Shaun's hands-on, adaptable teaching style ensures that each student receives personalized guidance, allowing them to progress at their own pace. His passion for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is evident in every class he teaches, and his dedication to his students' success is unwavering. Shaun's achievements and commitment serve as an inspiration to everyone at Huron BJJ, making him not just a coach, but a mentor and a role model.
