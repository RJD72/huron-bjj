24 Hour Gym Access

Exclusive to All Adult Members

24-Hour Access to Free Weights for Functional Strength Training

As part of our membership benefits, adult members with monthly memberships receive a personalized FOB key, granting them 24/7 access to our newly renovated strength and conditioning gym. In addition to the gym facilities, members enjoy access to our mat space, showers, and boxing equipment. Please note that martial arts or boxing sparring is not permitted in these areas. Our gym is equipped with a television with Internet access, so you can enjoy YouTube, Netflix, and other platforms while you work out.

Fully Equipped Weight Room for All-Day Powerlifting and Strength Building

Facilities and Equipment Include:

  • Machines: Treadmill, row machine, and more to meet your cardiovascular needs.
  • Turf Area: Complete with a weighted sled for dynamic training routines.
  • Strength Training: Two spacious squat racks, free weights, and all the equipment necessary for both beginner and advanced lifters.

Join Huron BJJ today and take advantage of the convenience of our 24/7 gym access, designed to support your fitness goals.
